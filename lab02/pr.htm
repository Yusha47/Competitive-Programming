    Office desks come in a range of shapes, sizes and designs. Let us take a look at some of the most common types of desks that you can get for your office:

    Writing Table

    If you are looking for a table that is simple yet elegant, you should try a writing desk. Whether you are crunching numbers, making a sales presentation, or writing a novel, this desk offers you the space to do all your work in a clutterless environment.

    Computer Table

    As the name suggests, computer desks are used for computing purposes. Available in a range of designs with adjustable heights, these types of desks are used by people who are constantly working on a desktop computer. This table comes with enough space to keep your monitor, keyboard and CPU. The table also comes with additional storage space for your files and other things.

    Corner Desk

    A corner table is a desk that is placed in the corner of the room. Thanks to its corner placement, this desk leaves enough space in the room for other uses. A corner desk, unlike a traditional table, comes with more leg space so that you can comfortably work with your legs stretched out. This would make an ideal office table if you are mostly working from home. Place it near a window for better natural light and a view.

    Conference Table

    Perhaps one of the most important types of office desks, a conference table is mostly used for meetings and presentations when the number of attendees is large. This type of table, depending on its size, can accommodate 5 to 25 number of people. It also comes with charging ports, facilities for a LAN cable, and more.

    Space

    Style

    Your office desk should represent your persona. Depending on whether you are looking for something that is traditionally styled or is contemporary, wooden desks give a more traditional feel, while desks made of steel and glass sections are more modern in style.
